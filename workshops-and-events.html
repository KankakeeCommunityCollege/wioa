Job Hunting in a Digital World

This workshop teaches you how to compete for employment in a digital job market. You will learn how to create your professional profile, utilize LinkedIn, use social media to promote your skills, retool your resume and reveal job resources.

Resume Development Class

This class teaches you the purpose of a resume and its basic parts. Individual instruction on resume creation will be offered and samples will be distributed. Computer literacy is recommended (students will use Microsoft Office Word to create their resume). Students should bring resume materials such as employment and education history.

Register for Resume Development

Open form in new tab or window: https://form.jotform.com/232574624722962

Resume Software & Resume Review

WinWay Resume software is available in the computer lab at the Kankakee Workforce Services office. Individuals can prepare a resume and receive up to five free copies a day on resume paper. The lab is open to the public. Resume review is conducted by trained staff every Tuesday from 1-3 p.m. on a walk-in basis.​​ ​
