Resume Development

This workshop teaches you the purpose of a Resume and it's basic parts. Learn how to craft your Resume to stand out in the stack. Upon completion of the workshop, feel free to utilize the Resource room to begin or improve your Resume draft. It is recommended to possess computer basic skills, as you will be utilizing Microsoft Work to create and edit your draft.

Register for Resume Development

Open form in new tab or window: https://form.jotform.com/232574624722962

Resume Software & Resume Review

Students and community members may obtain a free resume review by emailing their draft to careerservices@kcc.edu to receive courtesy feedback on how to improve their draft. Please note, we do not develop a resume for you, you must submit a draft with all your information to be reviewed. If you need assistance in developing your resume, you are welcome to use the resource room located at Kankakee Workforce Services and use the available software. ​
