Childcare Resource Fair

Finding the right childcare is a huge piece of the puzzle when you’re balancing school, work, and family life. Our Childcare Resource Fair is designed to connect parents and guardians directly with local licensed providers and the essential support services needed to make quality care accessible. Whether you are looking for current openings, need assistance navigating childcare subsidy applications, or want to explore early childhood career and funding pathways at KCC, this event brings all the experts into one room. Join us to bridge the gap between your career goals and the support your family deserves.

Date: Friday, March 27, 2026
Time: 10 a.m.-2 p.m.
Location: Kankakee Workforce Services, 450 N. Bradley Blvd, Bradley (across from Wendy's Restaurant)

Registration / Contact Information:

Resume Software & Resume Review

Students and community members may obtain a free resume review by emailing their draft to careerservices@kcc.edu to receive courtesy feedback on how to improve their draft. Please note, we do not develop a resume for you, you must submit a draft with all your information to be reviewed. If you need assistance in developing your resume, you are welcome to use the resource room located at Kankakee Workforce Services and use the available software. ​
