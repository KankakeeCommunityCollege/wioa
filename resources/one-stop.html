One stop employment and training services

There are 22 local partners in Illinois which drive this federally-funded network. Of the 22 partners, Kankakee Workforce Services is a three-county network of One-Stop Employment and Training Services, LWIOA 11. We provide resources to develop a skilled workforce to meet the needs of employers in today's changing and challenging economic environment.

Partners

Staff members of partner organizations that contribute to Kankakee Workforce Services can visit Partner Referrals. The Partners Referrals section of this website is meant for staff to make referrals to one of our partner organizations.
