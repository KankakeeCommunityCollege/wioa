Employment Services

IDES maintains Illinois Job Link, the free job exchange that connects job seekers to employers. Employment Service personnel can help individuals create an effective profile on https://illinoisjoblink.illinois.gov/

Veterans Services

Military veterans receive priority of service for all employment services.

Hiring Incentives

IDES connects employers with tax credits as an incentive to hire an economically disadvantaged individual. A fidelity bond is also available to employers who hire job applicants considered to be at risk due to their past life experiences.
