About Continuing Education and Career Services

The KCC Office of Continuing Education and Career Services responds to the specialized training and staff development needs of business, industry, public service and governmental agencies as well as providing personal enrichment courses for the community.

Among its services, the Office of Continuing Education and Career Services offers short-term and long-term employee development programs dealing with topics such as management techniques, organizational procedures, and skill enhancement.
